<p><ul><li>Sterilization Wrap</li><li>Autoclave CSR Wrap</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CSR Wrap, or central supply room wrap, is a specialized packaging material used for sterilizing medical instruments and supplies. The market is divided into two main types: sterilization wrap and autoclave CSR wrap. Sterilization wrap is designed to provide a barrier against contaminants while allowing steam penetration, ensuring effective sterilization. Autoclave CSR wrap is specifically engineered to withstand high-pressure steam used in autoclave sterilization processes. Both types are essential for maintaining the sterility and safety of medical supplies in healthcare settings.</p></p>
